Cognitive Science Project Participation

This study aims to explore the fascinating intersection between personality types and cognitive abilities, specifically focusing on facial recognition skills. We are interested in how different Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) personality types perform on the UNSW Face Test, a measure of facial recognition ability.

Participation involves taking two online tests: the MBTI personality test and the UNSW Face Test. Your contributions will be invaluable in advancing our understanding of cognitive science and personality psychology.

Please take the MBTI personality test and the UNSW Face Test before filling out this form. Your information will be used for research purposes only.
