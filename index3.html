Jun Huang

I am a scientist passionate about AI and brain science. Currently working at University of Bern, analyzing neural data to understand intelligence.

I received my PhD from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in Switzerland. My thesis focused on understanding the dopamine dynamics in reward-based learning.

In my free time I enjoy hiking in the Alps, skiing and photography.

My core research interests are at the intersection of neuroscience, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

Projects

Sound Alert App

A mobile application for sound classification and alerting. Yamnet is deployed on Android device to classify sound and alert user when a specific sound is detected. The app is built with Android Studio and the model is trained with Tensorflow.

Technologies: Android Studio, Tensorflow, Java, Python

Ghost Escape: Haunted Mansion

A 3D video game built with Unity and C#. The player is trapped in a haunted mansion and needs to escape from the ghost. There are 3 rooms with locked doors. The player needs to solve the puzzles to unlock the doors and escape from the mansion.

Technologies: Unity, C#
